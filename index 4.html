Main tumhari aankhon mein woh jadoo dekhta hoon, jo duniya ke sab rangon se zyada khubsurat hai. Tumhari muskurahat mere dil ko aise chhu leti hai, jaise chaand ki roshni raat ke andhere ko roshan kar deti hai. Tum meri zindagi mein ek nayi roshni le aayi ho, aur har pal tumhare saath guzarna mere liye ek khoobsurat sapna hai. Tumhe dekh kar, aisa lagta hai jaise maine apne har armaan ko haath se chhu liya ho.
